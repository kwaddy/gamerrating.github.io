Why Did I Do This?

Like I mentioned earlier, I made this website for a college course, however, there is a bit of a personal reason that I decided to create a website like this. Like many others across the world, I enjoy playing video games, but it was always extremely hard to find out whether a game was good or not from one specific resource. As such, I wanted to create a website that showcased reviews from multiple different sources. Think of it as a scholarly resource for games. This way, when people wish to look up and read about a game, they are able to see reviews from multiple different game sites like steam or the playstation plus store without having to specifically go to those websites. I also wanted to make something where others can leave reviews anonymously without having to attach their name to it at all. This may be cause for others to review bomb a specific game, but since this is a small project for the time being, I am not worrying about the review bombing just yet. I want to add ways for people to not just do text reviews, but upload video reviews as well. This will open up the site to a lot more variable reviews, but that will come at a later date once I work out the kinks of how to make sure that the reviews don't go into adult level ratings. This is a passion project and I will continuously work and update this as much as I can.
